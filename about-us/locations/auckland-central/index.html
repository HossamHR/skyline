Our Auckland Central branch offers specialist home renovation services throughout the region, from Herne Bay, Ponsonby, and Parnell to Stonefields, Greenlane, and Epsom.
SKYLINE ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS LTD was established to put homeowners first. Stressful (and expensive) complications within renovations generally occur because of miscommunication, poor planning, and disorganised trades. The SKYLINE ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS LTD approach changes that by prioritising a thorough planning process and transparent communication between all parties involved, resulting in a stress-free and enjoyable experience for homeowners — the way renovations should be. 
All our projects are led by a dedicated Renovation Consultant who takes care of everything and acts as a single point of contact for homeowners. With all the moving parts of a renovation, small things are often overlooked. Not with SKYLINE ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS LTD. Our Renovation Consultants bridge the gap between homeowners, contractors, and suppliers to create a streamlined avenue of communication. This way, homeowners are kept up-to-date with all the essential information pertaining to their project, without being overloaded with insignificant details.  
The initial consultation with your Renovation Consultant is a great opportunity to lay your cards on the table; everything from goals and dreams to budget and timeline. From there, they’ll provide you with concept drawings according to your discussion and a corresponding cost estimate. No work will commence until you’ve approved both. Once you have, all that’s left for you to do is relax and watch your vision come to life. 
“We always have confidence in SKYLINE ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS LTD because once a project is accepted they accompany the tradesman on the first visit, visit the site at intervals as the work progresses and visit again with the appropriate tradesman to sign off the work, ensuring at all stages that we are 100% satisfied.” — Jocelyn Kristensen, SKYLINE ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS LTD Client
There’s no hotter real estate market in Aotearoa than Auckland; the city offers so much natural beauty within an hour in any direction while also remaining a business and commerce hub. But with popularity comes competition. Climbing the property ladder is not easy but fixer-uppers are a great place to start. Many long-term homeowners are also choosing to improve their homes (and their property values) rather than pack up and move elsewhere. 
Our Renovation Consultants are local to Auckland Central, so if you’re unsure whether you need consent to reclad your Ponsonby heritage home, or to build a granny flat on your Mount Albert property, we’ll take care of it for you. Local knowledge and expertise also come in handy when it comes to finding reputable contractors. No more spending hours sifting through referrals and reviews. Each Renovation Consultant has a network of trusted industry professionals and suppliers that they consistently utilise on their projects because of their reliability and premium craftsmanship.
“SKYLINE ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS LTD helped us to achieve a really good renovation in trying times, with Covid putting stretches on resources. They were quick to help with any queries and were very professional and easy to deal with. We are very happy with our refreshed home.” — Ross and Michelle Baird, SKYLINE ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS LTD Clients
Our service has many components, but our overarching goal is to deliver homeowners fantastic renovations in a stress-free and enjoyable environment. All the SKYLINE ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS LTD processes have been developed with that mission in mind. Whether you’re interested in a bathroom, kitchen, or even a complete home renovation, trust the experts at SKYLINE ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS LTD to deliver your project on time and within budget.
