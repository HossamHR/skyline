How much will it cost to remodel my kitchen?

A feasibility assessment will need to be carried out in order to provide you with an accurate cost estimate for your project specifications. However, as a rough guide, it often costs around $30,000+ to remodel a kitchen. If you opt for luxury features, or if a significant amount of labor is required, costs will be higher. If you have a limited budget to work within, your kitchen remodeling specialist will work with you to produce a feasible design that works within your budget.

The costs provided are rough estimates and are subject to change.

Will I need a permit to remodel my kitchen?

Your kitchen renovation will most likely require a master permit, sub-permits and a Notice of Commencement. Further regulations may need to be adhered to if you live within a historic home. Your kitchen remodeling specialist can take care of any application processes required and will ensure your kitchen is updated in accordance with local regulations. 

What are some current trends in Houston kitchen design?

For the most part, Houston residents are opting to keep their kitchens traditional and elegant - with a few modern touches. In terms of layout, many homeowners are choosing to allocate a separate room of the house as a formal dining room, while the kitchen usually includes a casual and intimate dining area. Hardwood flooring and cabinetry is popular due to its character and warmth, while marble countertops are a common choice for benchtops and kitchen islands. Accent lighting usually includes recessed lights - as a final touch, some homeowners also like to include chandeliers. Whether you find inspiration in these trends or not, we encourage you to select kitchen features that will suit your long-term taste and lifestyle needs.

Our Expertise

SKYLINE ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS LTD’s process supports a bespoke design and build: a renovation tailored to your lifestyle requirements and taste. Your local Kitchen Renovation renovation consultant will manage every aspect for you - working to keep everything on track.

Step 1

Briefing / Initial Consultation

The first thing your Kitchen Renovation renovation consultant will want to know is what it is that you want to achieve through your home renovation. In this initial consultation, you can throw all of your ideas on the table - your consultant will then tailor them to your property and budget.

Step 2

Concept and Feasibility

With a clear understanding of your renovation goals, your Kitchen Renovation consultant can arrange for an architectural concept to be drawn up. This allows your Kitchen Renovation consultant to assess whether or not your ideas are feasible, and allows you to point out any changes you would like in the design - before any work begins.

Step 3

Working Drawings and Costing

Next, working drawings will be developed. These will take into account the engineering, structural, design and aesthetic decisions you have made. If you would like to make any further changes, now is the time to do so. From there, your Kitchen Renovation consultant will present you with a fixed-quote as well as a contract.

Step 4

Build / Construction Stage

Finally, you get to witness your project transition from the page to your property! Every aspect of the build will be managed for you, allowing you to carry on with your everyday life. Your Kitchen Renovation consultant will do everything in their power to keep your project on schedule so that it can be completed within a set timeframe and budget.

Step 5

Your Finished Renovation

The final stage is for your local Kitchen Renovation consultant to ensure that all Council documentation is completed and you have the necessary Code of Compliance documentation. Congratulations – your renovation is complete!
