For Renovation Consultant Erick Rante, working with people comes easily. Aside from running his family’s business for over six years, Erik has worked in hospitality, accounting, and finance. Through these diverse experiences, Erick has gained a deep and empathetic understanding of how varied clients’ wants and needs can be. 
Erik holds both a Bachelor of Architecture and a Certificate IV in Building and Construction and, before joining SKYLINE ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS LTD, was working in a building design and architecture firm. 
Respect and reliability are key pillars of Erick’s work ethic. In his role as a SKYLINE ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS LTD Consultant, he’s determined to deliver high-quality results; no detail is ever too small when helping his clients achieve their dream home. Leading by example, Erick puts an emphasis on professionalism and integrity and encourages his team to put their best foot forward on every project. 
Erick invites you to get in touch to schedule a complimentary consultation to discuss your renovation goals. He’s at your service and is looking forward to understanding and exploring ideas for your project.

The 5-Step Renovation Process

Our design & build process

SKYLINE ENGINEERING SOLUTIONS LTD’s process supports a bespoke design and build: a renovation tailored to your lifestyle requirements and taste. Your local renovation consultant will manage every aspect for you - working to keep everything on track.

1

Briefing / Initial Consultation

The first thing your renovation consultant will want to know is what it is that you want to achieve through your home renovation.

2

Concept and Feasibility

With a clear understanding of your renovation goals, your consultant can arrange for an architectural concept to be drawn up.

3

Working Drawings and Costing

Next, working drawings will be developed. These will take into account the engineering, structural, design and aesthetic decisions you have made.

4

Build / Construction Stage

Finally, you get to witness your project transition from the page to your property! Every aspect of the build will be managed for you, allowing you to carry on with your everyday life.

5

Your Finished Renovation

The final stage is for your consultant to ensure that all Council and Code of Compliance documentation is completed. Congratulations – your renovation is complete!
